Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 26, 2026

1. Introduction

ThreadConnect ("the Add-on") is a Gmail add-on developed by Juan Olivar ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use ThreadConnect.

2. Information We Access

ThreadConnect requests the following Gmail permissions solely to provide its core functionality:

  • Read email metadata and content – to extract subject-line IDs and message bodies from the thread you are currently viewing.
  • Search your mailbox – to find the output thread that shares the same ID.
  • Send replies on your behalf – to forward the matched content and selected attachments to the output thread via reply-all.

3. Data Storage

  • Templates are stored in your personal Google Apps Script User Properties. Only you can access them; they are tied to your Google account.
  • Rate-limiting counters are stored temporarily in Google Apps Script Cache Service and expire automatically.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

We do not:

  • Collect, store, or transmit your email content, attachments, or personal data to any external server.
  • Use analytics, tracking pixels, or third-party services.
  • Share any information with third parties.
  • Store or log email addresses, names, or any personally identifiable information.

5. No External Servers

ThreadConnect runs entirely within Google's infrastructure (Google Apps Script). No data ever leaves your Google account. There are no external API calls, databases, or servers involved.

6. Data Retention

Templates persist in your User Properties until you delete them. Cache entries (rate-limit counters, duplicate-send guards) expire automatically within 60 seconds. No other data is retained.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete any saved template at any time from within the add-on.
  • Revoke the add-on's access at any time via Google Account Permissions.
  • Uninstall the add-on from the Google Workspace Marketplace.

8. Children's Privacy

ThreadConnect is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
